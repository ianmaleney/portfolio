My name is Ian Maleney. I'm a writer and documentary producer, living in Ireland.

Recent projects include:

  • Producing The Witness: In His Own Words, winner of 'Podcast of the Year' at the Irish Podcast Awards and chosen as one of the twenty best podcasts of 2021 by The Guardian
  • Producing Crimeworld with Nicola Tallant, an award-winning crime podcast and sold-out live show
  • Editing Fallow Media, a unique and adventurous online literary journal

My first book, a collection of essays entitled Minor Monuments , was published in 2019 by Tramp Press. It was shortlisted for the Michel Deon Prize and the Butler Literary Award.

My writing has been widely published, including in The Guardian, Esquire, and the New Statesman. I was the Temple Bar Gallery & Studios writer-in-residence in 2020, and a recipient of the Arts Council's Next Generation Bursary Award in 2019. My agent is Tracy Bohan of the Wylie Agency.
