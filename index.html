My name is Ian Maleney. I'm a writer, radio producer, and web developer living in Dublin, Ireland.

My current projects include: producing The Stand with Eamon Dunphy and Crimeworld with Nicola Tallant, two of the most popular podcasts in Ireland; editing Fallow Media, an unique and adventurous unique online literary journal; building websites for great arts organisations like The Stinging Fly, The Dublin Review, Tramp Press, Paper Visual Art, and Skein Press.

My first book, a collection of essays entitled Minor Monuments, was published in 2019 by Tramp Press. It was shortlisted for the Michel Deon Prize and the Butler Literary Award. My writing has been widely published, including in The Guardian, Esquire, and the New Statesman. I was the Temple Bar Gallery & Studios writer-in-residence in 2020, and a recipient of the Arts Council's Next Generation Bursary Award in 2019. My agent is Tracy Bohan of the Wylie Agency.
